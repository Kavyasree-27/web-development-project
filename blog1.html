Gold Investment vs Stock Market Trading

By Nakul Saini

With the growing influence of social media, many individuals are being drawn to the stock market as a get-rich-quick scheme. But how safe is this path for the average person with little to no investment knowledge?

On the other hand, gold has quietly held its ground as a time-tested and stable investment, especially suited for beginners.

The Calculative Scam Behind IPOs

Many IPOs raise money at inflated valuations, only to see stock prices crash post-listing—leaving retail investors with huge losses.

Top 5 IPO Flops:

  • 1. Paytm – Dropped 60% after IPO.
  • 2. Zomato – Dropped 45% after surge.
  • 3. Nykaa – Fell by 30% post-IPO.
  • 4. CarTrade – Fell by 60% in first year.
  • 5. Suryoday Bank – Fell by 40%.

These IPOs are often promoted by influencers to create urgency and hype, only to trap investors in major losses.

Gold: A Timeless Investment Always in Your Favor

Gold isn’t a get-rich-quick scheme. It has weathered every storm and preserved value over centuries. For new investors, it's one of the safest assets.

It also acts as a hedge against inflation and economic instability. Unlike stocks, gold doesn't rely on startup hype or unpredictable markets.

The Hidden Agenda Behind Trading Hype

Many influencers and companies hype trading and IPOs only to profit off the public. These distractions are carefully crafted to steer people away from stable investments like gold.

The reality is most new traders lose money. The flashy success stories often cover up mass losses behind the scenes.

The Temptation of Stock Market Trading

A Dangerous Illusion

In recent years, social media influence has glorified trading. However, the reality is far from glamorous and often leads to heavy losses for uninformed investors.
